Hi, my name is Balint Pataki

I am an engineer-economist and developer from Hungary, who has experience amongst different industries.
I worked 5 years as data analyst in international forestry research projects, then I have been working in US procurement as a process lead analyst. I transitioned into programming on the job to help the actual company as I always concentrated on problem solving and higher efficiency.
My expertise is data science, visualization and process automation, however I like developing web applications as well. If I can connect them together, it is even better.
I always try to learn something new and never consider my knowledge as a finished article. Sharing is equally important for me, this is the way a team can develop - I always find some time to help junior colleagues.
